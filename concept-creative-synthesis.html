lightbulb What is Synthesis?

Unlike discriminative AI, which classifies data (e.g., "Is this a cat?"), generative AI focuses on synthesis. Creative Synthesis is the capability of a model to not just memorize training data, but to understand underlying patterns and traverse the 'latent space' to generate entirely novel combinations of concepts. It is the mathematical equivalent of imagination.

dataset

Ingestion

Models ingest massive datasets to learn the statistical probability of features.

Latent Mapping

Concepts are mapped as vectors in a multi-dimensional space called latent space.

Generation

New artifacts are created by sampling and decoding vectors from this space.

Training Data Ingestion

Vectorization

Complex data is compressed into lower-dimensional vector representations. "King" - "Man" + "Woman" ≈ "Queen".

Creative Synthesis

Upon prompting, the model samples the latent space, blending vectors to synthesize a new, original output that never existed in the training set.

Decoding & Refining

The chosen vectors are upscaled or decoded back into human-perceptible formats like pixels, waveforms, or tokens.
