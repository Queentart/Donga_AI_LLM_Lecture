Core Concepts

What is Generative AI?

Generative AI focuses on creating new content rather than just analyzing existing data. It distinguishes itself from discriminative AI by its ability to generate novel text, images, and code.

brush

Creative Synthesis

Algorithms that can generate new data instances rather than just categorizing them. Think of it as an artist painting a new picture from memory rather than a critic identifying who painted it.

folder_open

Beyond Classification

Moving from sorting data folders to creating original masterpieces. Traditional AI classifies "is this a cat?", while Generative AI draws a cat that never existed.

Understanding
Large Language Models

LLMs are trained on massive datasets to predict the next token in a sequence, enabling them to understand and generate human-like text through complex transformer architectures.

Read Whitepaper arrow_forward

Transformers Architecture

The neural network backbone allowing parallel processing of data sequences, revolutionizing how AI understands context.

Token Prediction

Probabilistic modeling to guess the next word in a sentence based on context, temperature, and training weights.
